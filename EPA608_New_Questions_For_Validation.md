## Question 1 — Core

**Question**

A worker attaches gauges to a stationary air-conditioning system only to check operating pressures. Under Section 608, why must that worker be certified?

**Answer choices**

A. Attaching and detaching gauges can release refrigerant
B. Reading a pressure gauge changes the refrigerant type
C. Certification is required for all electrical measurements
D. Gauges may only be owned by certified wholesalers

**Correct Answer**

Attaching and detaching gauges can release refrigerant

**Explanation**

EPA treats attaching or detaching hoses and gauges as refrigerant-handling activity because it can release refrigerant from the appliance.
